Our Services

Pentadbir Harta (Wasi) is an individual or organization appointed by the testator to carry out the trust and manage the estate as desired by the testator after death. The estate administrator must ensure the estate is managed/divided well among the beneficiaries.

  • Providing consultation related to estate administration.
  • Preparing will documents, hibah, and trust deeds.
  • Offering services as estate administrators (Wasi).
  • Executing will declarations.
  • Obtaining probate from the High Court.
  • Ensuring the estate is well-managed as desired by the testator.
